Good morrow fellow peers, pursuers of knowledge, truth and the like. I, Samantha Morgan, progeny of the Cockwill pedigree, who has endured great perils of nature and conflicts of man throughout the parted past, am confident in prevailing at my current task at hand. I am a well- fortified wall flower, haphazardly wrought. I have testified to the truth and come out on top My adversary attempts to portray itself as paradise; the fooled one perceives its blinding light as bliss. The enemy is common to both you and I alike, but its disguise, for some, is not as readily recognized. Like a well-seeming friend, it can misguide and distort; distracting you until you forget to retort. But I will triumph over irreverent intent instilling passion for free thinking as clear as Thors- laughter. The rival will restrain you from using your mind to its best But I shall smother it and call you from your mental rest. An appreciation for intellect will arise from the slaughter And I shall have conquered the foe long sought after.
