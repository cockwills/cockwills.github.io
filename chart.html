Classification Types

Enumerative
- Based on an alphabetical list of subject headings,which are then assigned numbers according to their subdivisions.
Hierarchical
- Based on a tree structure,arranged from the most general to the most specific within a topic.
Faceted
-Based on seperate classifications of each text for each one of its different properties, which combind to give a unique reference number.
